Dependencies
A dependency denotes:

the owner of the dependency (the system making the declaration)
the system that is depended 'on'
the nature of the relationship (it is 'for' some 'data')
the direction of the data flow, aka the mode, which may be 'r' (read), 'w' (write), or 'rw' (read/write)
the protocol (e.g. http, https, s3, etc)
